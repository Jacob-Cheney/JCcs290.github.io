This assignment was difficult for me because it was the synthesis of essentially everything we've learned thus far.
Making both the server and local files work together was probably the most difficult part for me since handlebars and express-handlebars have a lot of small details that aren't very explicit in nature unless the full documentation is understood well with examples
The professors lecture code and examples were very helpful for me, and piazza was an especially important resource to me for this assignment. I surprisingly didn't use a lot of extraneous resources besides our class this time around, just because of how specific the needs were for this assignment.
